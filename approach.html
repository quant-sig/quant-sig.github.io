Approach

We're going to start by implementing simple models (e.g delta hedging) on index funds to build an intuition on markets and the implementation details, and move onto targeting specific industries, commodities, and markets.


We aim to do the boring things, the things that people don't do either because it's not sexy or because it's tedious. We can't possibly compete with big firms via brainpower or capital, so that's what we're doing differently.
