About Us

We are QuantSIG, a Special Interest Group of CompSoc at the University of Edinburgh, founded in 2023. We host meetings and events related to finance, machine learning and are interested in anything tech-related. Join our Discord to find out about where and when we meet and chat to current members!

In the past we have participated in competitions such as Optiver - Trading at the Close where our team received a bronze medal. Our members also participate in non-finanance related competitions such as the AI Mathematical Olympiad.
